# Meeting Cadence

> Meeting schedule and communication channels for the DocLens AI engineering squads.

---

## Scheduled Meetings

### Daily Standup
- **When:** Monday–Friday at 10:00 AM (15 Minutes)
- **Who:** All squad members, led by [[Project Management]].
- **Purpose:** Focuses on immediate progress updates, current daily tasks, and identifying technical blockers.

### Weekly Planning Sync
- **When:** Mondays at 11:00 AM (45 Minutes)
- **Who:** Squad leads, shared services lead, project manager, and [[Engineering Head]].
- **Purpose:** Tracks sprint milestones, coordinates deliverables across teams, and assigns resources from the [[Buffer Reserve]].

### Sprint Demo & Retrospective
- **When:** Every second Friday at 3:00 PM (1 Hour)
- **Who:** All project members.
- **Purpose:** Demos completed features, gathers team feedback, and discusses improvements for the next sprint.

---

## Communication Guidelines

- **Primary Chat Channels:** Use dedicated team chat channels for daily queries (e.g., `#squad-a-extraction`, `#squad-b-translation`, `#squad-c-tts`).
- **Technical Discussions:** Document architectural decisions and API modifications in the team knowledge base rather than in chat history.
- **Escalations:** Use the [[Escalation Matrix]] to route blockers to the correct team.
