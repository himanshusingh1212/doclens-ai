# Terminology Management

> **Squad:** [[Squad B — Translation]]  
> **Operational Depth:** Depth 2 (Custom glossary tasks)

---

## Purpose

**Terminology Management** specialists maintain project glossaries, term lists, and translation memories to ensure consistent terminology.

---

## Responsibilities

- **Glossary Management:** Builds and updates glossary tables across target languages.
- **Translation Memory:** Integrates previous translation mappings to speed up processing and ensure consistency.
- **Formatting Validation:** Checks translations to ensure style rules are followed.
