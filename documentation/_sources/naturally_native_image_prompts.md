# Naturally Native Image Prompts

You can use these prompts in Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or other AI image generators to create the assets for your new website.

### 1. Hero Image (`assets/img/hero-img.png`)

**Dimensions:** 16:9 or Square
**Prompt:** `A beautiful, rustic arrangement of traditional Indian sweets including Besan Laddoo, Kaju Katli, and Gulab Jamun on a vintage brass plate. Placed on a dark wooden table with scattered marigold flowers, pistachio shavings, and a small clay diya lit in the background. Warm, golden hour lighting, cinematic food photography, highly detailed, inviting and appetizing.`

### 2. About Page Hero (`assets/img/about-2.jpg`)

**Dimensions:** 4:3
**Prompt:** `An elderly Indian grandmother with a gentle smile, wearing a traditional cotton saree, sitting in a rustic village kitchen, rolling fresh besan laddoos by hand. Warm sunlight streaming through a window, illuminating floating flour dust. Authentic, heartwarming, cinematic, national geographic style photography, earthy tones, green and cream color palette.`

### 3. Kitchen/Gallery Image (`assets/img/gallery-1.jpg`)

**Dimensions:** 1:1 or 4:3
**Prompt:** `Close-up shot of pure golden desi ghee melting in a large traditional cast-iron kadhai over a soft flame. Steaming hot and bubbling gently, rich and vibrant colors. Food photography, macro lens, shallow depth of field, warm and comforting atmosphere.`

### 4. Diwali Special Event (`assets/img/events-1.jpg`)

**Dimensions:** 3:2
**Prompt:** `A premium, eco-friendly gift box made of raw natural kraft paper, tied with a simple jute string. Inside the box are neatly arranged Kaju Katli and Kesar Pedas. The box is placed on a green banana leaf with lit diyas and marigold garlands around it. Festive Indian aesthetics, premium branding, dark and moody lighting, highly detailed.`

### 5. Menu Items (e.g., Kaju Katli)

**Dimensions:** 1:1 (Transparent Background)
**Prompt:** `A single perfect piece of Kaju Katli with shimmering silver vark, placed on a clean white background. Studio lighting, highly detailed food photography, soft shadows, 8k resolution. (Optional: Use a background removal tool later).`

### Tips for Best Results:

- Use the **seed** of an image you like to generate the other images in the same style.
- Include the keywords `earthy tones`, `warm lighting`, and `rustic` to match the `#3a8f3a` (forest green) and `#fdf8f0` (cream) palette of your website.
