# Buffer Reserve

> **Focus Area:** Agile Resource Allocation  
> **Team Size:** 3 Members  
> **Allocation Control:** [[Engineering Head]]

---

## Purpose

The **Buffer Reserve** is a flexible group of developers allocated dynamically to support squads with high workloads, helping teams hit sprint goals and resolve critical path blockers.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- **Agile Support:** Temporarily joins squads to help complete critical sprint tasks.
- **Cross-Functional Tasks:** Resolves technical blockers that cross squad boundaries.
- **Code Coverage:** Helps squads manage sudden workload increases or resource shortages.

---

## Allocation Workflow

1. Squad leads identify resource shortages during weekly planning syncs.
2. Leads request support from the [[Engineering Head]].
3. The Engineering Head assigns developers from the Buffer Reserve based on project priorities.
