# Engineering Head

> **Team Role:** Technical Leadership  
> **Contact Escalation:** Level 3 (Final escalation point)

---

## Purpose

The **Engineering Head** is responsible for the overall technical architecture, engineering practices, and delivery of the DocLens AI project. They guide technology choices, ensure alignment between squads, and resolve cross-team blockers.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- **Technical Direction:** Defines the overall system architecture, including client-side processing targets, storage schemas, and API design.
- **Resource Management:** Assigns engineering resources across Squads A, B, and C, and manages the allocation of the [[Buffer Reserve]].
- **Delivery Management:** Partners with [[Project Management]] to align technical delivery with product milestones.
- **System Stability:** Sets performance standards, security requirements, and quality guidelines.

---

## Team Size & Structure

Direct reports include:

- 3 Squad Leads ([[Squad A — PDF Extraction]], [[Squad B — Translation]], [[Squad C — TTS]])
- Lead Project Manager ([[Project Management]])
- Shared Services Lead ([[Shared Services]])
