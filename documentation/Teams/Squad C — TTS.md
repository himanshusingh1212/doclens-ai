# Squad C — TTS

> **Focus Area:** Text-to-Speech Synthesis and Audio Rendering  
> **Team Size:** 10 Members (1 Lead, 9 Engineers)  
> **Primary Pipeline:** [[TTS Pipeline]]

---

## Purpose

**Squad C** manages the audio synthesis features. They handle local WASM voice generation, browser-native speech interfaces, and audio quality checks.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- **Voice Synthesis:** Synthesizes page text using offline WASM and browser-native engines.
- **Voice Catalog:** Manages voice files and offline installation workflows.
- **Audio Controls:** Coordinates paragraph and sentence-level audio highlighting and navigation.
- **Audio Verification:** Tests synthesized audio quality, pronunciation, and performance.
